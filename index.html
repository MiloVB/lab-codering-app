meet up with ur friends

Club Loom is a place where you can easily meet up with friends, hang out, and take a break from your busy school day. Everyone is welcome, whether you come alone or with others.

You can also ask us for comic strips, board games, or other fun activities. We’re always happy to help you find something to play or read, and to make your time at school more enjoyable.
